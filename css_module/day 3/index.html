Background Colors and Images in Design

Creative ways to enhance your web design

1. Solid Background Colors

Solid background colors can create a clean and professional look. Use colors that match your brand identity or theme.

2. Gradient Backgrounds

Gradient backgrounds add depth and interest. Combine complementary colors to make your design stand out.

3. Using Images as Backgrounds

Images as backgrounds can set a specific mood or theme. Ensure the image is high quality and doesn't overpower the content.

4. Framed Images

Use framed images to highlight important content or visuals.
